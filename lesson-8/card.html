Squirrel

In Greek, the word “squirrel” means “shadow tail". January 21 is National Squirrel Appreciation Day!

  • Scientific name: Sciuridae
  • Lifespan: 15-18 years
  • Size (length): Ranges greatly by species, from 3-36 inches (7-91 cm)
  • Habitat: Wooded areas, on every continent except Antarctica and Australia

The clever Sciuridae family (highly varied with 285 species like the flying squirrel) were purposely introduced to US city parks to create “a bucolic atmosphere”. To prevent teeth wear from gnawing on nuts, their front four continue to grow throughout their lifetime. When threatened, squirrels run away in a zigzag pattern (a strategy to escape predators), but it also leads to discombobulated drivers!
